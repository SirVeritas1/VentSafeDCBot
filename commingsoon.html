VentSafe

What is VentSafe?

VentSafe is a free-to-use Discord bot that helps your members vent and confess in a safe, anonymous way. VentSafe does not have a premium version because we at VentSafe believe that there should not be a price on mental health.

How can you get VentSafe?

Right now, VentSafe is still in the testing stages and is not available for use in your server. We sadly do not take tester applications at this moment either.

What are our plans for VentSafe?

Our plan is to hopefully make VentSafe a popular bot that can help a lot of people around the world vent and get into a better mental state.

We really want to work with authorized therapists to make VentSafe the best it can be!

You are not alone. If you need support, consider reaching out to a free therapist online.
